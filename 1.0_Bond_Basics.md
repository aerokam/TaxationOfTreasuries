# 1.0 Bond Basics

## Dependencies
**None** (foundation layer)

---

## Scope
In this specification, "bond" refers to U.S. Treasury securities (bills, notes, bonds, TIPS)

---

## Core Terms

**Face Value (Par):** $1,000 per bond

**Coupon Rate:** Fixed annual interest rate set at issuance
- Expressed as decimal to 3+ places (e.g., 0.02125 = 2.125%)
- Treasury coupons in increments of 0.125% (1/8 percent)

**Semi-Annual Payment:** Interest paid twice per year
```
semiAnnualPayment = faceValue * couponRate / 2
```

**Annual Interest per Bond:**
```
annualInterest = faceValue * couponRate
```

**Price:** Market value expressed as percentage of par (e.g., 102.5 = 102.5% of par)

**Settlement:** Trade date + 1 business day (T+1) for secondary market trades

**Maturity Date:** Date when principal is repaid to bondholder

**Last-Year Interest Payments:**
- Coupon payments are always semi-annual (every 6 months)
- If maturity date falls within 6 months of prior payment: 1 payment in final year
- If maturity date falls more than 6 months after prior payment: 2 payments in final year
